Why This Course?

We believe that empathy, iterative learning, and problem-solving are fundamental human traits, not exclusive talents reserved for "creatives".

This course is designed to demystify Design Thinking—strip it of academic jargon, and teach it as a biological instinct that you can harness deliberately to solve complex problems in the real world.

🛠️

The Execution

Translating wicked problems into actionable, prototyped solutions.
