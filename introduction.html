02: The Process in Action

The Sugar Conflict

Johnny's Dilemma

Johnny wants sugar but needs to avoid punishment. He goes through a process:

  • 1. Clarity: Understanding he's caught.
  • 2. Ideation: Swallowing sugar? Lying?
  • 3. Execution: The "Ha-ha-ha" distraction.

This mirrors the design process: Clarity ‚Üí Ideation ‚Üí Execution.

05: The Framework

The Four Pillars

Empathy

Gaining Clarity. Understanding the user's problem as your own.

Ideation

Looking for Options. Brainstorming multiple paths.

Execution

Prototyping. Implementing the best idea.

Iteration

Feedback Loop. Testing and improving based on results.

06: Conclusion

The Road Less Traveled

Design is the ability to take the road less traveled to find innovative solutions. It isn't about never failing; it's about the ability to revise and try again.
