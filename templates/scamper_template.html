SCAMPER Ideation Template

Design Thinking — Divergent Ideation Phase

Use the SCAMPER prompts below to force lateral thinking on your current subject. Don't worry about feasibility yet.
S

Substitute

What materials, rules, or people can be swapped out?

C

Combine

What happens if we merge this with another idea or feature?

A

Adapt

What else is like this? How can we copy from a different industry?

M

Modify / Magnify / Minify

Can we change the scale, shape, color, or emphasis?

P

Put to another use

Who else could use this? In what other context?

E

Eliminate

What can be removed to simplify the solution?

R

Rearrange / Reverse

What happens if we flip the sequence or reverse the roles?
