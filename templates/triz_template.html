TRIZ Ideation Template

Design Thinking — Theory of Inventive Problem Solving

TRIZ operates on the premise that engineering or design problems often involve a trade-off (contradiction). Define what you want to improve, and what gets worse as a result.
The Contradiction Match

Improving Parameter (+)

What specific feature or metric do you want to get better?

Worsening Parameter (-)

What collateral damage happens when you improve the above?

Applying Inventive Principles

Cross-reference your contradiction in a TRIZ matrix to find suggested principles, or brainstorm using these common universally-successful principles:

1. Segmentation / Fragmentation Can you break the object or system into smaller, independent parts?
2. Asymmetry Can you change the shape from symmetrical to asymmetrical?
3. Nesting (Russian Dolls) Can you place one object inside another?
4. Skipping / Blessing in Disguise Can you conduct a high-speed process to bypass a harmful phase, or turn a harm into a benefit?
